## Tips for tricky Italian/Portuguese pairs
- **burro** = butter in Italian, but "burro" = donkey in Portuguese!
- **prego** = you're welcome / go ahead (not "I pray")
- **la moglie** = wife (not intuitive from Portuguese "esposa")
- **il nonno / la nonna** = grandfather / grandmother (similar to Portuguese "vovô/vovó")
