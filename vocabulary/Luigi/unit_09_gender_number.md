# Unit 9 — Use concordância de gênero e número (Gender and number agreement)

## Articles

| Type | Masculine singular | Feminine singular | Masculine plural | Feminine plural |
|------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Definite (the) | il / lo / l' | la / l' | i / gli | le |
| Indefinite (a/an) | un / uno | una / un' | — | — |

- **il** → most masculine nouns: il libro, il gatto
- **lo** → masculine nouns starting with s+consonant, z, gn, ps: lo studente, lo zaino
- **l'** → nouns starting with a vowel: l'amico, l'ora
